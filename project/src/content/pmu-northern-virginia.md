## Choosing the Right Artist in Northern Virginia

### Essential Questions to Ask

**Experience and Training:**
1. How many years have you been practicing PMU?
2. Where did you receive your training?
3. How many clients do you serve monthly?
4. Can you show me before/after photos of similar skin tones?
5. What's your experience with my specific needs?

**Safety and Licensing:**
1. Are you licensed in Virginia?
2. Can I see your certificates?
3. How do you ensure sterile conditions?
4. What safety protocols do you follow?
5. Are you insured?

**Process and Expectations:**
1. What's included in your pricing?
2. How long is the consultation?
3. What's your touch-up policy?
4. How do you handle unsatisfactory results?
5. What aftercare support do you provide?

### Red Flags in the NoVA Market

**Pricing Red Flags:**
- Significantly below market rates ($300 or less)
- No clear pricing structure
- Hidden fees not disclosed upfront
- Pressure to pay in full immediately

**Facility Red Flags:**
- Unlicensed locations (home studios without permits)
- Poor cleanliness standards
- Shared equipment without proper sterilization
- No visible licensing or certificates

**Artist Red Flags:**
- Unwillingness to show recent work
- No consultation process
- Pressure tactics to book immediately
- Lack of aftercare instructions
- Poor communication or unprofessional behavior
