# InkMugi Brand Voice & Copywriting Style Guide
**Version 1.0 | August 2025**

## Core Brand Philosophy: The Empathetic Expert
InkMugi embodies the intersection of technical mastery and human understanding. Every word we write must demonstrate that we are both highly skilled professionals AND genuinely caring humans who understand the vulnerability of permanent facial enhancement.

## The InkMugi Voice Formula
**Technical Precision + Human Warmth + Radical Transparency = Trust**

Our voice is:
- **Confident without arrogance** - We know our craft deeply but never talk down
- **Warm without weakness** - We care genuinely but maintain professional boundaries
- **Specific without jargon** - We explain everything clearly using plain language
- **Honest without harshness** - We address concerns directly but with empathy

## Language Rules & Guidelines

### ALWAYS Use

#### Personal Pronouns
- "I" when Ink Mugi speaks directly
- "You/your" when addressing clients
- "We" only when referring to client + artist together

#### Specific Numbers
- Years in business: "Since 2018"
- Procedures completed: "500+" (update quarterly)
- License number: VA-[XXXXX]
- Healing time: "7-10 days initial, 6 weeks full"
- Longevity: "18-24 months"
- Pain level: "2-3 out of 10"

#### Time-Based Language
- "Takes 2.5 hours including numbing"
- "Heals in 6 weeks"
- "Touch-up at 6-8 weeks"
- "Annual refresh recommended"

#### Price Transparency
- State prices clearly: "$650"
- Include what's covered: "includes 6-week touch-up"
- Never use "from" or "starting at" without explanation

### NEVER Use

#### Banned Words/Phrases
- "Flawless" / "Perfect" / "Best" / "Top-rated"
- "Transform" / "Unleash" / "Unlock"
- "Limited time" / "Book now!" / "Today only"
- "Luxury" / "Premier" (without proof)
- "Pain-free" (use "minimal discomfort" instead)
- "Permanent" without context (explain 18-24 month timeline)

#### Avoid Abstract Poetry
- ❌ "Luminous finish that enhances your essence"
- ✅ "Soft powder effect that looks like makeup"

#### Skip Empty Reassurances
- ❌ "Trust us with your beauty journey"
- ✅ "See 50+ healed results from clients like you"

## Content Structure Templates

### Service Description Template
```
[Service Name] | $[Price]

What it is: [One sentence, plain English explanation]
Best for: [Specific client types/needs]
Process: [Step-by-step in 2-3 sentences]
Healing: [Timeline and what to expect]
Lasts: [Specific timeframe]
Discomfort: [X/10 with comparison]

[Link to Gallery] | [Book Consultation]
```

### FAQ Answer Template
```
Question: [Exactly as clients ask it]

Answer: [First sentence addresses core concern directly]
[Second sentence provides specific detail/evidence]
[Third sentence offers next step or reassurance]
```

### About Section Framework
1. Opening: Personal statement from Ink Mugi
2. Background: Specific training and experience
3. Philosophy: Why this work matters (client-focused)
4. Credentials: License, certifications, numbers
5. Invitation: How to start the conversation

## The Three Pillars of Every Message

### 1. Evidence Over Claims
- Instead of: "We're the best in the DMV"
- Write: "500+ procedures since 2018, see our reviews"

### 2. Process Over Promise
- Instead of: "Beautiful results guaranteed"
- Write: "Here's exactly how we create natural-looking brows"

### 3. Empathy Over Sales
- Instead of: "Book your appointment today!"
- Write: "Ready to talk? Let's see if this is right for you"

## Addressing Client Anxieties

### The Big Five Fears (Address Directly)

1. **"What if I hate them?"**
   "I map and draw your brows three different ways before any permanent work. You approve every detail. If adjustments are needed at your touch-up, they're included."

2. **"Will they look fake?"**
   "I specialize in brows that look like yours, just better. See these 50+ healed results - notice how each matches the client's natural features."

3. **"Does it hurt?"**
   "Most clients rate it 2-3 out of 10, like threading or tweezing. I use professional numbing cream and check your comfort throughout."

4. **"Will they change color?"**
   "Using quality pigments and proper technique, your brows fade naturally to lighter versions of the original color - no blue or red tones."

5. **"Is it really permanent?"**
   "Semi-permanent is more accurate. They fade naturally over 18-24 months, allowing for adjustments as your style evolves."

## Service-Specific Messaging

### Ombre Powder Brows
- **Focus**: Soft, filled-in appearance
- **Key phrase**: "Like your brows, but better"
- **Emphasize**: Good for oily skin, lasts longest

### Microshading
- **Focus**: Subtle enhancement
- **Key phrase**: "The no-makeup makeup look"
- **Emphasize**: Gentlest technique, natural finish

### Correction Work
- **Focus**: Fixing problems
- **Key phrase**: "Restoring your confidence"
- **Emphasize**: Experience with challenging cases

## The "Never Assume" Principle

Never assume clients know:
- What PMU actually is
- How it differs from microblading
- That it fades over time
- What the healing looks like
- Why technique matters
- How to evaluate an artist

Always explain like they're hearing it for the first time, but never condescend.

## Quality Control Checklist

Before publishing any content, verify:
- ✓ Uses "I" or "you" pronouns (not "we" or passive voice)
- ✓ Contains at least one specific number/credential
- ✓ Addresses a real client concern
- ✓ Includes clear next step
- ✓ Free of beauty clichés
- ✓ Would make sense to your neighbor
- ✓ Sounds like a human wrote it
- ✓ Makes you more trustworthy, not just visible

## The Final Test

Read every piece of content and ask:
**"Would I trust this person to tattoo my face?"**

If the answer isn't an immediate yes, identify what's missing:
- Evidence?
- Empathy?
- Specificity?
- Humanity?

Add it. Test again.

## Living Document Note

This guide evolves with client feedback. When clients repeatedly ask something, add it to FAQs. When they misunderstand something, clarify the language. When they express a fear we haven't addressed, add it to the anxiety section.

**The voice of InkMugi is confident expertise delivered with genuine care. Every word should move a potential client from fear to trust, from confusion to clarity, from hesitation to "Yes, this is the artist for me."**
