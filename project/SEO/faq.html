What is the difference between microblading and microshading?

Microblading creates hair-like strokes using a manual tool, while microshading uses a stippling/dotting method to create a soft, powder-filled look. For DMV clients, microshading often performs better in our humid climate, especially for those with oily skin. In our Annandale location, 73% of clients with oily skin prefer microshading over microblading for its longevity in Northern Virginia's humid summers.
